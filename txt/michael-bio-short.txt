Michael is a research staff member at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center. His
research interests are in automated planning and autonomous systems, integration
of planning and reinforcement learning. Michael's planners have won numerous
awards. Michael is a member of ICAPS executive council. He was ICAPS2021 program
co-chair, organizer of PRL and HSDIP workshops. He was a lecturer at ICAPS2013
Summer School. He regularly serves as SPC for ICAPS, IJCAI and AAAI.
